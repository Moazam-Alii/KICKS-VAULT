Frequently Asked Questions (QNA)

❓ How do I place a bid?

Go to any listed sneaker's page, enter your wallet address and bid amount in SOL, select Trade or Delivery, then click “Place Bid”.

⏳ What happens after the bidding ends?

Once the bid ends, the highest bidder is notified by email and has 2 hours to pay. The seller is also informed to prepare the sneaker for shipment or verification.

📦 When will my sneaker be listed again if I choose ReTrade?

If you selected Trade and your sneaker passes human verification, it will be re-listed in 3 days.

💸 What if no one bids on my sneaker?

If your sneaker receives no bids, it will be removed after expiry and you’ll get an email update.

🕵️ How is sneaker authenticity verified?

All traded or delivered sneakers are human-verified. Fake or unverifiable items are returned, and buyer funds are refunded if needed.
